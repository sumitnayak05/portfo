001 : SMART DUSTBIN

ABOUT THE PROJECT

We all are aware of the increasing population of our country . With increase in population we have increase in garbage . Dealing with garbage specially in urban areas is a huge task and here is the use of smart dustbins. The usual dustbins requires to be operated by processing foot against its lever and then throwing garbage , on the other hand , the smart dustbins does it by itself . The smart dustbin can be regarded as a multipurpose dustbin as it can also be used to detect the level of the garbage . As the dustbin gets filled by the garbage we hear a buzzer alarm and the LED light glows ,indicating that the dustbin needs to be emptied. Thus it will be great to use it in the waste management as well.

INTRODUCTION

The smart dustbin uses the new idea of automation and monitoring of waste management operation. As waste management operations all over the world attempt to become more cleaner and greener, Smart dustbin uses the ultrasonic sensors along with motor drivers and control assembly to improve and smoothen ground level mechanism for waste collection

BENIFIT OF SMART DUSTBIN

The LED Display System for public information helps to regularize the garbage waste management properly. It will stop overflowing of dustbin along roadsides. The filling and cleaning time of smart bin will also be reduced thereby making empty and clean dustbins available to common people. It aims at creating a clean environment. It will help us to take a step forward towards SWACHH BHARAT ABHIYAN

SAVE MANPOWER

Smart dustbin can help to save manpower by notifying the workers to collect the dustbin only when the dustbin is full which is indicated by the buzzer sound and the LED lights..
